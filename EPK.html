Biography

28Ø8 (pronounced “twenty-eight-oh-eight”) is an independent metal band based in Collingwood, Ontario. The group is active in the local Southern Ontario music scene, performing a style that blends elements of groove metal and death-core. They are currently working on their Debut album, and are finalizing the release of their first single "panic Attack" in 2026.

The band, having solidified their line-up in the summer of 2025 with vocalist Gabriel Snow, Drummer Henry Doyle, Bassist/Guitarist Zac Weymark, Guitarist Ronin MacIntyre, and Guitarist/Bassist Brendan Gonsalves. They started playing live and building a community in the Collingwood and Wasaga Beach area at small bars like "Taps n' Tunes" and "Studs Lonigan's Pub", schools, and backyards. Also getting the attention of some friends who own a recoding studio, to start recording all the music they had been working on. In December 2025 with the help of Robert from CHS productions they put together a very successful concert opening for 'The Social Era' filling up the venue and selling most of the homemade merch.

In the beginning of 2026 28Ø8 started the year off strong by securing some of their biggest shows in Cities far from home, the first show they kicked off with after a writing and recording hiatus, being in Newmarket At 'The Spot' with bands; Loose Jaw, Neon Lux, Ravens Haven, Whiskey Jack Where they were also covered in an article by Loud TO Magazine.

28Ø8 is currently getting ready to release their first single "Panic Attack", they're constantly refining their setlist/live show, and working with many different friends (promoters, bands, fans) to bring their community continued fun live experiences, and recorded music for everyone to enjoy.

"2808 opened the show with their brand of metal-core thrash. The members met in high school in Collingwood and have been together for a few years making a lot of noise on the hard core scene. Members Henry Doyle on drums, Zac W and Ronin M on guitar, Brendan Gonsalves on bass and lead singer Gabriel Snow looked and sounded like seasoned pros, encouraging the audience to squeeze in tight and slam to the beat." - Micheal Drukarsh (LoudTO)

Tristan Leynes

( Full on Sessions )

"Meet 2808 — a band raised right. From the moment I arrived, it was obvious someone taught these wholesome kids to be polite, grounded, and genuinely helpful. This was a big shoot, and I had load of recording gear to carry. Before I could even ask, they were ready to grab equipment and help me carry things to the basement. - Tristan Leynes "Full on Sessions""

"And what a basement. A proper rehearsal bunker built for high gain — real guitar amps stacked along the walls, PA speakers, a full drum kit, acoustic treatment everywhere, and stage lights beaming colour across the room. I could tell by the shelves of CDs and records that at least one previous generation of headbangers had clearly shaken these walls." - Tristan Leynes "Full on Sessions"

"I suggested they play as quietly as possible so they could hear each other and optimize the recording. That lasted… maybe thirty seconds. This is a band with only one true setting: loud. Loud in a good way — energetic, passionate, and completely committed to the moment." - Tristan Leynes "Full on Sessions"

"2808 love what they do, and it shows. They’re tight, fearless, and already carrying themselves like the next wave of Metal bands coming up fast." - Tristan Leynes "Full on Sessions"
