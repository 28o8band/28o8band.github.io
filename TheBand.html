About The Band!

Larry the Lobster is the founder of 28Ø8...

28Ø8 (pronounced “twenty-eight-oh-eight”) is an independent metal band based in Collingwood, Ontario. The group is active in the local Southern Ontario music scene, performing a style that blends elements of groove metal and death-core

28Ø8 was formed in a Collingwood highschool by...
.
Henry Doyle known for his thunderous fast blasts, and insane machine gun double kick action.
.
Zac Weymark with his roboticaly percise technical ability on the bass/guitar and his creative songwriting.
.
Ronin MacIntyre bringing the intricate rhythmns of the deep to hold down the song. Also creating tasteful harmonies, enhancing the sound to a whole new level.
.
.
They soon introduced...
.
Gabriel Snow ex soundcloud rapper who chanels ancient forces through his terrifying screams or solem singing.
.
Brendan Gonsalves, last to join the line-up, a versitile musician who channels the legends of the past into the future.
.
